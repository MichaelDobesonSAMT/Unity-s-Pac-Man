1.2:
Pac-Man is a well-known videogame which has stood the test of time and cemented itself has a classic.
In this game the player has to control Pac-Man in a maze that has a pill on every cell and the players goal is to eat all the pills.
In the meantime there are 4 ghosts following the player and if they catch you you lose a life. If the player loses al his lifes the game is over.
There are also super-pills in the maze which make Pac-Man invincible and capable of eaing the ghosts.
In our variation of Pac-Man there is only one ghost and the maze is randomly generated each game.
